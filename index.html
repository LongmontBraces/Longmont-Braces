About Longmont Braces

At Longmont Braces, board-certified orthodontists Dr. Stephanie Ross and Dr. Aden Peterson are passionate about creating healthy, confident smiles that last a lifetime. We’re a warm, fun, and patient-focused practice where everyone is treated like family—not a number. From the moment you walk in, you’ll feel the difference: a welcoming team that truly listens, runs on time, and puts your comfort and goals first.


Whether you’re looking for traditional braces, clear braces, Invisalign®, or ClearCorrect® clear aligners, we offer the latest options customized to fit your lifestyle and budget. An amazing smile is one of the best ways to express yourself—let us help you put your very best one forward!Ready for a smile you’ll love? Visit us at longmontbraces.com to learn more and schedule your complimentary consultation today!

Our Treatment Philosophy

We believe every smile tells a story. From teen orthodontics and adult orthodontics to Invisalign® and Invisalign Teen®, plus early intervention for children, we tailor treatment plans to fit your lifestyle, goals, and budget. Our focus is not just straight teeth — it’s lifelong oral health, confidence, and comfort throughout your journey.

Why Patients Choose Us

With state-of-the-art digital scanning, flexible payment plans, and a team certified in the latest orthodontic techniques, Longmont Braces stands out as a trusted partner in your smile transformation. We’re proud to serve families across Longmont, Lyons, Hygiene, Berthoud, Niwot, and Gunbarrel with teen orthodontics, adult orthodontics, Invisalign®, Invisalign Teen®, clear aligners, and braces for kids.
