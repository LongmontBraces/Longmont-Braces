About Longmont Braces

Longmont Braces is a premier orthodontic practice dedicated to creating confident, healthy smiles for patients of all ages in Longmont and surrounding Colorado communities. Our experienced team combines advanced technology with personalized care to deliver exceptional results in a warm, welcoming environment.

Our Treatment Philosophy

We believe every smile tells a story. From traditional braces to Invisalign® and early intervention for children, we tailor treatment plans to fit your lifestyle, goals, and budget. Our focus is not just straight teeth — it’s lifelong oral health, confidence, and comfort throughout your journey.

Why Patients Choose Us

With state-of-the-art digital scanning, flexible payment plans, and a team certified in the latest orthodontic techniques, Longmont Braces stands out as a trusted partner in your smile transformation. We’re proud to serve families across Longmont, Firestone, Mead, and beyond.
